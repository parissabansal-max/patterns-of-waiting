You sit in the grass.

Blankets overlap, phones glow, someone laughs.

The sky leaks from blue into pink into violet.

Forty minutes pass.

Time feels fast and slow at the same time.

The speakers hum. Someone hums with them.

Everyone refreshes their feeds for “ON STAGE NOW.”
